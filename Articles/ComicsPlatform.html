Thanks for downloading this app ! Comixviewer is a first step in the journey of building a new comics platform , one that you'll be very happy to use. We envision a future where there are no ads to interrupt you from reading comics. Just a purchase at the cost of a cup of coffee is all you need to get started on this adventure. Initially we will be providing all the public domain content , carefully curated and enhanced for you .

We are also working on a Comics Creator and Comics Publisher apps , that will complete this ecosystem for you. Just like you tube for videos, this aims to be the go to location for creating and publishing comics. Sounds interesting ? Like it or not, by installing this app, you are already a fellow member of this community, and there are so many ways you can help to make this project a success! After all , no project ever succeeds without YOU !

There will be several iterations of this app , adding additional features over time . Please bear with us and help us make this app better for you . Everyone wins in the end ! You are always encouraged to email any ideas or issues that you encounter while using the app to our email at comicsviewerapp@gmail.com.
